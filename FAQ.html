Frequently Asked Questions - Super Search

Is my personal data safe?

Super Search extension and Super Search website will never access, request, or capture any of your personally identifiable details. Our properties are created to keep all of your data anonymous and 100% safe.

How do I remove your Google Chrome extensions?

If you wish to remove one of our Google Chrome extensions, just follow these steps:

  1. Go to Settings in Google Chrome.
  2. Click “Extensions” in the menu on the left.
  3. Find the extension you wish to remove and click “Remove”.
